# Usability Test Plan

## Objective
To evaluate the user experience of the Strangers Calendar App and identify areas for improvement.

## Participants
- 10 users with varying levels of technical expertise
- Different devices (desktop, mobile)

## Tasks
1. Create a new calendar
2. Set availability windows
3. Share calendars with others

## Procedure
1. Recruit participants and provide instructions
2. Observe users while they complete the tasks
3. Collect feedback through surveys and interviews

## Analysis
- Document user interactions and comments
- Identify common pain points and areas for improvement
